Workflow

How AI Receptionist handles your calls

1

Answer + Identify

A new patient calls. AI Receptionist answers instantly with your clinic name, greets them naturally, and identifies why they are calling.

2

Intake Questions

It asks the right questions: reason for visit, preferred times, whether they have been seen before, insurance details if relevant, and any other info you define.

3

Book + Confirm + Remind

The patient gets booked into your calendar. Confirmation SMS goes out immediately. Reminders follow to reduce no-shows.

Sample conversation
AI: "Thanks for calling Greenwood Physio. How can I help you today?"
Caller: "I have been having lower back pain. Can I see someone this week?"
AI: "Of course. Have you been to our clinic before?"
Caller: "No, this would be my first visit."
AI: "No problem. I have Thursday at 10am or Friday at 2pm available. Which works better for you?"
